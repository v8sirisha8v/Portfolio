Here's my experience with this event!

`

The event allowed me to meet plenty of successful business women, and inspired me to take on more leadership roles instead of shying away. At the beginning, we had a speaker who emphasized how we shouldn't apologize for being too strong-voiced, and that anyone of us could be leaders. This changed my point of view on my career. I had aspired to be an Augmented Reality programmer, but having my own ideas to impact the world, that would be amazing. I didn't have to be a follower. Later on, I attended the Emerging Technologies Workshop, where we were free to talk about our different ideas that could change the world. We were introduced to numerous emerging technologies, one being Augmented Reality. Our speakers discussed Pokemon Go , and the different applications Augmented Reality could have on our life, giving me ideas on what I could do to further my interest in this industry. I learned that Niantic, the creator of Pokemon Go , is a wonderful company that I could find opportunities in. Most of all, through this experience, I gained confidence that I could turn my ideas into something impactful, instead of being a common worker for a company.
